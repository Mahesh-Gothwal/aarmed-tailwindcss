Benefits of Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® for Manufacturing Companies

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® is a powerful probiotic strain that offers numerous advantages for manufacturing companies. Below are some key benefits that make it a superior choice for various industries.

Survivability

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® is extremely resilient, maintaining its potency even under harsh conditions such as high heat, extreme pH levels, and pressure. This ensures that the probiotic remains active and effective from production to final use.

No Microencapsulation

Unlike many probiotics, Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® does not require microencapsulation for protection. This simplifies the manufacturing process, reduces costs, and ensures that the probiotic remains effective without additional processing steps.

Resilience

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® is known for its high resilience, surviving environmental stressors such as freezing, drying, and fluctuating temperatures. This ensures it remains viable during manufacturing, transport, and storage.

Safe

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® has a long history of safe use in food and pharmaceutical applications. It is non-toxic and poses no significant risk to human health, making it an ideal ingredient for a wide range of products.

Viability

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® maintains its viability even in long-term storage conditions without the need for refrigeration. This makes it highly flexible in manufacturing and logistics, reducing storage-related challenges.

Temperature Resistant

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® can withstand extreme temperature changes, from freezing to high heat, without losing efficacy. This makes it ideal for manufacturing processes that involve variable temperatures.

What is Bacillus clausii UBBC-07®?

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® is a robust and highly resilient probiotic strain, offering numerous benefits across diverse industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, and agriculture. Its ability to survive harsh environmental conditions makes it one of the most reliable probiotics for enhancing gut health and improving product performance.

Unlike other probiotics, Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® does not require microencapsulation, which makes it a cost-effective solution for manufacturers. With excellent heat resistance, pH fluctuation tolerance, and unmatched survivability, it's the ideal choice for dietary supplements, functional foods, animal feed, and agricultural products.

Bacillus clausii UBBC-07® also plays a vital role in supporting digestive health by balancing the beneficial bacteria in the gut. Its immune-boosting properties and overall health benefits make it a trusted ingredient for manufacturers focused on safe, effective, and high-quality health products.
