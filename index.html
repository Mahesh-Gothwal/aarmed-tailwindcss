About Aarmed Formulations

Aarmed Formulations Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in 2000 with an ambition to become a leading provider of healthcare products & services in India, situated in Moraiya, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, and beyond. Aarmed Formulations is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified organization driven by technology, quality, and service. We believe that health is wealth, and health is everything.

Specializing in the development and manufacturing of probiotic strains and their formulations, Aarmed Formulations is one of the largest probiotic manufacturing companies in India. We have gained expertise and capabilities in providing high-quality probiotic solutions aimed at improving health and wellness through microbiota management.

We are in the service of health and are committed to ensuring world-class service through our quality formulations.
